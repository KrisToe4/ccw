On-site tech support

Kristofor is available to come to your house and help you out with any computer questions you may have. He has experience ranging from complete beginners attempting to upload pictures and send copies to relatives to professionals needing to set up a business LAN.
His basic rate is $50 per hour (min. 1 hour) within the South Cowichan Valley (Malahat to Duncan). There may be additional travel fees outside of that area. Whether you need a one-time service or are looking for ongoing maintenance, Kristofor can help. Gift certificates are available. An initial 15-minute consultation via phone or email is free of charge. Current clients also receive 15 minutes free email or phone support when necessary for minor issues that may come up. While he strives to finish all work on-site within your appointment time frame, certain tasks are better suited to off-site work. For a short list of some other services he provides, click here.
